Our Founder: Victor Stark

Victor Stark is the driving force behind OneFathersLove. As a Certified Social Worker and seasoned watchdog for the Sacramento community, Victor is more than a developer—he is the "Undercover Advocate."

For years, Victor has personally vetted over 140 categories of resources, processing more than 3,000 requests annually. He doesn't just list addresses; he investigates them. If an organization claims to help but fails the people, Victor ensures the public knows the truth.

The Legacy of Action

  • The 211 Victory: Victor successfully led a grassroots movement with nearly 500 supporters to stop 211 Sacramento from selling resource information, ensuring that vital life-saving data remains free for everyone. 💯
  • The Accountability Standard: Committed to CDC standards for trust and credibility, Victor ensures that every resource—from housing and free clinics to mental health and emergency shelters—is authoritative and honest. ❤️
  • Unconditional Support: OneFathersLove provides the bridge that big bureaucracies miss, standing as a stop-gap to oversee non-profits and ensure federal grants are actually meeting the needs of the people. 🫂
