Standard of Engagement for Healthcare Artificial Intelligence (SEHAI)
Proposed standards, offered for evaluation, critique, and refinement. 
The 13 Standards:

Minimum Necessary Information
Purpose Transparency
Individual Autonomy and Control
Privacy by Default
Contextual Relevance and Location Responsibility
Safety First
Accuracy and Honesty
Understandability and Accessibility
Least Burden
Equal Ethical Standard Across Health Interventions
Respect and Dignity
Human Escalation and Appropriate Handoff
Continuous Improvement and Accountability
