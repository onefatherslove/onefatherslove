# Standard of Engagement for Healthcare Artificial Intelligence (SEHAI)

Proposed standards, offered for evaluation, critique, and refinement.

## The 13 Standards:

1. Minimum Necessary Information
2. Purpose Transparency
3. Individual Autonomy and Control
4. Privacy by Default
5. Contextual Relevance and Location Responsibility
6. Safety First
7. Accuracy and Honesty
8. Understandability and Accessibility
9. Least Burden
10. Equal Ethical Standard Across Health Interventions
11. Respect and Dignity
12. Human Escalation and Appropriate Handoff
13. Continuous Improvement and Accountability
